Cup Of Coffee Latte Leaf

Price: Rs.150

Description:

Cup Of Coffee Latte Leaf is a delightful beverage that combines the rich flavors of coffee with the creamy texture of milk, topped with a delicate leaf-shaped foam.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso coffee
  • Steamed milk
  • Milk foam

Preparation Method:

Start by brewing a shot of espresso coffee using your preferred method.
Steam the milk until it reaches a creamy texture, ensuring not to overheat.
Pour the steamed milk over the espresso in a cup, holding back the foam with a spoon.
Spoon a dollop of milk foam onto the surface of the latte, creating a leaf shape or any desired design.

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy Cup Of Coffee Latte Leaf as a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon treat.
Serve in a clear glass mug to showcase the beautiful layers of coffee, milk, and foam.

Unique Characteristics:

The leaf-shaped foam adds a whimsical touch to the classic latte.
The combination of espresso coffee and steamed milk creates a smooth and indulgent beverage.
Cup Of Coffee Latte Leaf is customizable, allowing you to adjust the coffee-to-milk ratio to suit your taste preferences.

Benefits:

Provides a caffeine boost to help you stay alert and focused.
The milk in the latte contributes to your daily calcium intake.
Enjoying a Cup Of Coffee Latte Leaf can be a comforting and enjoyable ritual, perfect for moments of relaxation or socializing.
