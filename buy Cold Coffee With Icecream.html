Cold Coffee With Ice Cream

Price: Rs.200

Description:

Cold Coffee with Ice Cream is a delightful beverage that combines the rich flavors of coffee with the creamy indulgence of ice cream. Whether enjoyed as a refreshing treat on a hot summer day or as a comforting pick-me-up, this drink never fails to satisfy both coffee and dessert lovers alike.

Ingredients:

  • Strong brewed coffee or espresso
  • Milk (dairy or non-dairy alternatives such as almond milk, oat milk, etc.)
  • Ice cubes
  • Vanilla ice cream (or any flavor of your choice)
  • Sweetener (sugar, honey, or syrup) - optional
  • Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (optional, for garnish)

Preparation Method:

1. Brew a strong cup of coffee or espresso and let it cool to room temperature.
2. In a blender, combine the cooled coffee, milk, ice cubes, and sweetener (if desired). Blend until smooth and frothy.
3. Scoop a generous serving of vanilla ice cream into a tall glass.
4. Pour the blended coffee mixture over the ice cream.
5. Optional: Top with a dollop of whipped cream and sprinkle with chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for an extra indulgent touch.
6. Insert a straw and serve immediately.

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy Cold Coffee with Ice Cream as a dessert alternative or a decadent afternoon treat.
Pair it with cookies, brownies, or cake for an extra special indulgence.
Serve it at brunch gatherings or as a delightful addition to your coffee bar offerings at parties.
