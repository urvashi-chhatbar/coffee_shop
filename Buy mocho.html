Mocha

Price: Rs.300

Description:

Mocha is a delicious coffee beverage that combines espresso with steamed milk and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream. It offers a perfect balance of rich coffee flavor and sweet chocolate indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso coffee
  • Steamed milk
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream

Preparation Method:

Start by brewing a shot of espresso coffee using your preferred method.
Steam the milk until it reaches a creamy texture, ensuring not to overheat.
Mix chocolate syrup into the espresso.
Pour the steamed milk over the chocolate espresso mixture.
Top with whipped cream for a delightful finishing touch.

Serving Suggestions:

Enjoy Mocha as a comforting treat on a chilly day or as a decadent dessert coffee.
Serve in a warm mug for the perfect cozy experience.

Benefits:

Provides a delightful combination of caffeine and chocolate flavor.
The milk in the mocha contributes to your daily calcium intake.
Enjoying a Mocha can be a satisfying and indulgent experience, perfect for uplifting your mood.
