Irish Coffee

Price: Rs.250

Description:

Irish Coffee is a classic cocktail made with hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and topped with whipped cream. It's a warming and indulgent beverage often enjoyed as a dessert or after-dinner drink. The drink has a history dating back to the 1940s and is associated with Foynes Airbase in County Limerick, Ireland.

Ingredients:

  • Hot brewed coffee
  • Irish whiskey
  • Sugar (brown or white)
  • Heavy cream (for whipped cream topping)

Preparation Method:

1. Preheat a heat-resistant glass or mug.
2. Dissolve a teaspoon of sugar in the glass with a little hot coffee.
3. Pour in a shot of Irish whiskey and stir.
4. Fill the glass with hot brewed coffee.
5. Top with whipped cream, gently floating it on the surface.
6. Optionally garnish with nutmeg or cocoa powder.

Variations:

While the classic recipe remains popular, there are several variations of Irish Coffee, including Baileys Irish Coffee, Nutty Irishman, and Irish Coffee Martini.
